What We Do

RefrMe has developed its own innovative use of technology to deliver its technical, marketing and customer services. Our platform allows us to outsource our internal processes to independent business owners. Our independent business owner operations have three departments.

  • We outsource our technical and support contracts to qualified technicians to install broadband internet to our customers on a self-employed independent business owner model.
  • We outsource our customer service to talented work at home agents, our agents are under our independent business owner model. Agents are recruited based on experience and are given training on utilizing our platform and the technologies that we have created to generate an income. Our agents dictate their own working hours and receive training in entrepreneurship and how to run their own business.
  • We also offer the opportunity to reward our customers annuity rewards for successful referral's to go with our company philosophy and model. Our business is built to offer as many opportunities as possible for our users to generate an income online.
