About Us

We provide telecommunication solutions such as internet fibre to the Home and to business with our partnerships with MTN, Mweb and Bitco, as well as RefrMe fixed-LTE solutions with our own brand of Wi-fi routers. We’re excited to be in the space we’re in as Internet broadband is a billion-rand industry in South Africa, the existing national infrastructure is considered the best in the continent and the South African telecoms industry is considered one of the fastest growing in the world.

Internet fibre optics is the current trend because of its increased benefits compared to traditional Internet broadband solutions.

Fibre Internet is currently being deployed across South Africa with only a 30 ℅ coverage across the country. All the major South African telecoms companies are moving away from traditional broadband solutions such as ADSL and shift their infrastructure to fibre optic internet, including Telkom which announced it will no longer be providing ADSL internet packages.

Only 3 million out of 17.5 million households in south Africa currently have internet fibre and it is expected that the number will continue by half a million households on a yearly basis since all network providers in the country migrate their infrastructure from traditional ADSL copper wires to fibre optic infrastructure which is why RefrMe has entered partnership with the major fibre network providers
